SLCC 360: From Vision To Action

February 13, 2026 | Redwood Campus

A Letter from the President

Welcome to SLCC 360, an exciting day designed to bring our institution together around our shared vision: to create an Ideal Student Learning Experience where every student can succeed.

This conference brings together faculty, staff, and leaders from across all our campuses to collaborate on the strategic initiatives that define our college. Today, we'll explore how each of us—in our unique roles—contributes to student success and institutional excellence.

From vision to action, we're committed to turning aspirations into tangible outcomes. Through collaborative breakout sessions, engaging discussions, and meaningful connections, we'll sharpen our focus on what matters most: our students and our collective impact.

Thank you for being here. Your participation matters.

Ideal Student Learning Experience (ISLE)

Closing Plenary: Ideal Student Learning Experience

1:00 – 2:00 PM | Student Center

About This Session

This unique closing plenary features SLCC's Provost Dr. Jamie Cooper and other institutional leaders unveiling and walking the audience through the Ideal Student Learning Experience.

This is a pivotal moment in our institutional journey. The ISLE framework represents our collective vision for what an exceptional learning experience looks like at SLCC—one where students are supported, engaged, and empowered to succeed across all modalities and settings.

Join us as we explore the key components of ISLE and discuss how each of us contributes to creating these ideal experiences for our students.

Key Focus Areas

  • ✓ How relationship-rich education transforms student success
  • ✓ Creating equitable and engaging learning environments
  • ✓ Supporting student completion and pathways to success
  • ✓ Implementing ISLE principles across all SLCC campuses

Coming Soon: Full details of the ISLE framework, including components, learning outcomes, and implementation strategies will be shared during this session.
